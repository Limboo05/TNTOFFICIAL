Common Questions

Frequently Asked

Do I need any prior sewing experience to enroll?
Not at all. Our Foundations of Sewing program is designed for absolute beginners. You'll start from the very basics — how to operate a sewing machine, understanding fabrics, and making your first stitches. Many of our most successful graduates started with zero experience.
What is the class schedule like?
We offer both weekday (Monday–Thursday, 10am–2pm) and weekend (Saturday & Sunday, 9am–3pm) schedules. You can choose what fits your life. Some programs also offer evening slots. Schedule selection happens during your consultation call.
Can I pay in installments?
Yes! We offer flexible payment plans — split your tuition into 2 or 3 installments depending on the program. A minimum deposit of 40% is required to secure your spot, and the balance is spread across the program duration.
Are materials and equipment provided?
Yes, all basic materials and tools are included in your tuition — fabrics for practice projects, threads, needles, measuring tools, and access to our industrial machines. For the Diploma tier, you also receive a personal toolkit to keep after graduation.
What certificate will I receive upon completion?
You'll receive a TNB Fashion Academy Certificate upon completing each program. Diploma students receive the TNB Fashion Design Diploma — a comprehensive credential recognized across the Nigerian fashion industry. All certificates include a unique verification code for authenticity checks.
Do you offer online classes or remote learning options?
Our core programs are in-person at our Lagos studio because fashion design is fundamentally hands-on. However, the Fashion Business Mastery program includes a hybrid option with online sessions for business modules, combined with in-person workshops for practical components.
