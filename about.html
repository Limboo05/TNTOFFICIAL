Our Beginning

A Dream Sewn by Hand

Trendy N Bespoke started in 2014, not in a glamorous studio, but in a small room in Lagos with a single industrial sewing machine and an unshakable belief: that Nigerian fashion could stand shoulder to shoulder with any in the world.

What began as a one-person operation — sketching designs by night, sewing by day, delivering orders on okadas — has grown into a full fashion house with a team of over 20 creatives, a training academy, and a client base that spans Nigeria and beyond.

"I didn't start TNB to be famous. I started it because I looked at what Nigerian designers were doing and thought — we can do better. We MUST do better."

The name itself tells the story. Trendy — because we stay ahead of what's next. N — because Nigeria is the core of everything we create. Bespoke — because every piece we make is intentionally crafted for the person who will wear it.

The Visionary

Meet the Founder

Founder & Creative Director Tobiloba Okonkwo

Tobiloba Okonkwo

Founder & Creative Director

Tobiloba's journey into fashion was unconventional. With a background in architecture and no formal fashion training, she brought a designer's eye for structure, proportion, and detail into garment construction — and it changed everything.

After years of designing for friends and family, a viral Asoebi order in 2013 became the catalyst to turn a passion project into a business. TNB was officially registered in 2014, and the rest is a story written in stitches, lace, and unrelenting drive.

Today, Tobiloba leads every collection's creative direction, mentors TNB Academy students, and advocates for Nigerian fashion on panels, podcasts, and international showcases.

Our Journey

Milestones That Defined Us

From a single room to a fashion house — every year added a new chapter to our story.

2014
The First Stitch
TNB was officially registered. One room, one machine, one dream. First client order: a custom Asoebi set for 15 people.
2016
First Bridal Collection
Launched our dedicated bridal line. Within 3 months, we had a 6-week waitlist for bridal orders.
2018
The Studio Move
Moved into our first proper studio in Victoria Island. Team grew to 8 people. Launched Ready-to-Wear.
2020
Resilience Year
Navigated the pandemic by launching online consultations, mask production, and virtual showcase.
2022
TNB Academy Launched
Opened our training arm. First cohort: 24 students. Answering years of requests from aspiring designers.
2024
1,000th Graduate
TNB Academy hit a milestone: over 1,000 students trained across Nigeria.
2026
The Next Chapter
Expanding our studio, launching e-commerce, preparing for international showcase. The best is yet to come.

The Process

How We Create

Every TNB piece goes through a deliberate process — no shortcuts, no compromises.

01

What We Believe

Our Philosophy

These principles shape every decision — from which fabrics we buy to how we treat our team.

Heritage First
We build on Nigerian textile traditions — Adire, Aso-Oke, Ankara — not as novelty, but as foundation.
Precision Always
We obsess over seams, hems, finishes like a watchmaker obsesses over movement.
Client as Co-Creator
We don't dictate — we collaborate. Your vision, our expertise, one authentic piece.
People Over Profit
We pay fair wages, source ethically, price honestly. Building to last.
Growing the Ecosystem
Through our Academy, we train future competitors. A rising tide lifts all boats.
Global Mindset, Local Roots
We study global trends but everything is unmistakably, unapologetically Nigerian.
